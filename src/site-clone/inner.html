Frequently asked questions

Will you really get me a job?+

No. Trabajo Listo is not an employment agency. What we offer is a professional American-style resume, interview preparation, and job-search strategy. The final outcome depends on your experience, your effort, and the job market. We operate on a "best effort" basis.

How fast will my resume be ready?+

The Review package ($49) is delivered within 48 hours. The Professional package ($249) takes 3 to 5 business days. The Premium ($549) can take 7 to 10 days because of the included consulting.

Can I request changes if I don't like it?+

Yes. Every package includes at least one round of revisions. The Premium package includes multiple rounds until you're fully satisfied.

Do you handle specific industries?+

Yes. We have worked with clients in construction, hospitality, healthcare, tech, education, transportation, retail, and professional services. We tailor the resume to your industry.

Is my information safe?+

Yes. All your information is protected with SSL encryption and we never share your data with third parties. We comply with U.S. privacy regulations.
